Quick Installation

Our modular elevators fit through standard doorways and ceilings, enabling installation in as little as one day.

Energy Usage

Our elevators use minimal electricity, comparable to a dishwasher, and run on a standard 220v, 15-amp outlet.

Quiet

Unlike conventional elevators, lifts use a quiet electric motor, requiring no hydraulics, vacuums, or machine rooms. They plug into a standard outlet.

Our Products

The Duo Alta combines sleek design, compact functionality, and style. Its curved structure and transparent panels complement any décor, adding light and openness. More than stylish, it offers smooth, convenient floor-to-floor movement, enhancing your home's sophistication and practicality.

The Trio Alta Wheelchair Lift is a compact, stylish mobility solution for wheelchairs, walkers, and rollators. It ensures full home accessibility with advanced features like hold-to-run controls, a light curtain, and thru-car design. It plugs into a 220v outlet and fits 13.5 sqft, carrying wheelchairs, heavy items, or up to three people, enhancing freedom and independence.

The Duo Classic is our original Home Elevator, carrying two people between floors in under 30 seconds. Its dual-rail technology allows double-entry or through-car access, ensuring unmatched convenience. With stylish design and customizable options, the Duo Classic offers a practical, elegant home mobility solution.
